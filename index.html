Welcome to Wild Rescues, a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and releasing injured, orphaned, and displaced wildlife.

We are a group of licensed wildlife rehabilitators, trained in the care of Florida native wildlife. We are committed to providing professional care to sick, injured, and orphaned animals, with the goal of releasing them back into their natural habitat.

Wild Rescues is supported by a network of volunteers who help with animal care, fundraising, outreach, and administration.
